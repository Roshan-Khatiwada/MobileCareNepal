About MobileCare Nepal

MobileCare Nepal is a professional smartphone repair service provider in Kathmandu, Nepal, offering trusted mobile repair services across the country.

Who We Are

Experienced Mobile Repair Technicians

MobileCare Nepal was established by experienced technicians who gained technical knowledge from international training institutions in Bangalore and have years of practical experience in the mobile repair industry.

After working with different mobile repair companies, our founder started MobileCare Nepal to provide professional, transparent, and trustworthy smartphone repair services for Nepali customers, repairing hundreds of iPhones and Android phones every month.

Our Mission

To provide fast, affordable, and reliable mobile repair services in Nepal so customers can quickly get their devices back in working condition.

Our Core Values

We believe in customer satisfaction, quality repair service, transparency in pricing and process, and strong warranty support for peace of mind.

Our Vision & Experience

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted mobile repair service providers in Nepal. Our technicians have experience repairing iPhones, Android smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, and mobile hardware and software using professional diagnostic tools and high-quality replacement parts.

Professional mobile repair service in Nepal for iPhone and Android smartphones.
